Welcome to our cupcake shop!

Here is the recipe of how we make our secret cupcakes that are juicy delicous!

  1. 2 cups flour
  2. 1/2 teaspoon salt.
  3. 2 teaspoons baking powder.
  4. 5 cups, softened butter.
  5. 4 cups sugar (if you like your cupcakes very sweet, add a little more.)
  6. 2 eggs.
  7. 1 cup milk.
  8. 1 teaspoon vanilla essence (optional)
